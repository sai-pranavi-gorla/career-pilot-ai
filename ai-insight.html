🤖 Artificial Intelligence

Complete Guide to Becoming an AI Engineer

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that enables computers to learn, reason and make decisions. AI powers chatbots, recommendation systems, self-driving vehicles, voice assistants and modern automation tools.

What Does an AI Engineer Do?

AI Engineers build intelligent applications that can learn from data and solve real-world problems. They create machine learning models, train AI systems and improve automation processes.

Required Skills

  • Python Programming
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • TensorFlow / PyTorch
  • Data Analysis
  • Mathematics & Statistics
  • Problem Solving

Salary in India

Mid-Level: ₹12–20 LPA

Popular AI Career Roles

Future Scope

AI is one of the fastest-growing industries in the world. Future opportunities include Generative AI, Robotics, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Healthcare AI and Autonomous Systems.

Career Growth Path
