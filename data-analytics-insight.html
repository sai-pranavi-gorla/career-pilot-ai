📊 Data Analytics

Complete Guide to Becoming a Data Analyst

What is Data Analytics?

Data Analytics is the process of collecting, cleaning, transforming and analyzing data to help businesses make better decisions. Companies use data analytics to understand customer behavior, improve products and increase profits.

What Does a Data Analyst Do?

A Data Analyst gathers data from multiple sources, cleans the data, creates reports, dashboards and visualizations, and helps management make informed business decisions.

Required Skills

  • Excel
  • SQL
  • Power BI
  • Python
  • Data Visualization
  • Statistics

Salary in India

Fresher: ₹5–8 LPA
Mid-Level: ₹8–15 LPA
Experienced: ₹15–30+ LPA

Top Companies Hiring Data Analysts

Google, Amazon, Deloitte, Accenture, Infosys, TCS, Wipro and Capgemini.

Recommended Certifications

  • Google Data Analytics Certificate
  • Microsoft Power BI Certification
  • IBM Data Analyst Professional Certificate
  • Tableau Certification

Future Scope

Data Analytics is one of the fastest-growing careers. With experience, professionals can move into Data Science, Business Intelligence, Data Engineering and AI Analytics roles.

Career Growth Path

Data Analyst → Senior Data Analyst → Business Analyst → Data Scientist → Analytics Manager
